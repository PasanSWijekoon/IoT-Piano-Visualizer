About Us

The IoT Piano LED Visualizer is an innovative project designed to revolutionize piano learning by integrating technology and real-time feedback mechanisms. Our platform combines a Casio SA-45 keyboard, RGB LED strip, push button sensors, and an ESP32 microcontroller with Wi-Fi connectivity to create an interactive and engaging learning environment.

With two distinct modes—Tutorial Mode and Play Mode—the IoT Piano LED Visualizer offers a user-friendly approach to piano practice, guiding learners through visual LED cues to improve their skills efficiently. The project aims to address common challenges faced by piano learners, such as the lack of immediate feedback and engaging practice methods, by providing an intuitive, real-time feedback system that enhances user engagement and learning outcomes.

Developed as a part of our passion for combining technology with education, the IoT Piano LED Visualizer not only offers an immersive learning experience but also contributes to the growing field of educational technology. By leveraging readily available hardware and open-source software, our system is designed to be affordable and accessible for piano learners of all levels.

Join us on our mission to transform the way people learn music, making it more interactive, enjoyable, and effective through cutting-edge IoT solutions.
